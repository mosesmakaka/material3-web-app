M3 Expressive

Feel the Expression

Material 3 Expressive turns a static interface into something responsive, emotive and inclusive. It applies dynamic color schemes to personalize an app's look and feel, introduces motion physics for natural interactions, and refreshes shapes to harmonize with typography.

Get started

Key features

Dynamic color

Choose your own accent color and let the system generate an accessible palette. Dynamic color adapts brand colors to user preferences for a unique experience.

Motion physics

Animations feel alive, fluid and natural thanks to the new motion physics system. It replaces traditional easing curves and durations with spring‑like dynamics that react to user input.

Shapes & typography

The shape system has been overhauled with dozens of new shapes and harmonized corner radii. Combined with the modern type scale, this creates rhythm and hierarchy.

Inclusivity & accessibility

Every element in Material 3 Expressive is built with accessibility in mind. Colours, motion and components are designed to work for as many people as possible.
